SMS consentEmployees

This page documents the opt-in workflow and the exact verbal consent script used for internal AMREF operational and incident-related SMS alerts. This is strictly non-marketing and intended for employee on-call/incident notification.

Purpose

AMREF sends low-volume SMS messages to employees for urgent operational and incident response notifications (e.g., system outages, critical process alarms, or time-sensitive operational events).

Message type
Operational / Incident alerts only
Estimated frequency
Rare (typically < 100/month; often far less)
Audience
Internal employees only
Marketing content
None

Opt-in workflow (verbal)

Employees are invited to opt in during onboarding or during on-call / incident-response setup. Participation is optional. Consent is recorded internally (date/time, employee name, and phone number) and can be withdrawn at any time.

Important: Consent is not a condition of employment. Employees may opt out by replying STOP.

Verbal consent script

The following script is read verbatim to each employee:

"You agree to receive operational and incident-related SMS messages from AMREF. Message frequency is limited to urgent operational alerts only. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance. Consent is not a condition of employment."

After the employee agrees, AMREF sends a confirmation message to the phone number provided. Opt-out requests are honored immediately.

Sample messages

Operational alert: AMREF Alert: Checklist Completed.

Incident alert: AMREF Alert: Quality Test Failure detected.

Help message: AMREF Alerts: Internal operational notifications only. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg & data rates may apply.

Opt-in confirmation: AMREF Alerts: You are subscribed to operational SMS notifications. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance.
